Frequently Asked Questions

Is listing really free?
Yes — completely free. No listing fees, no success fees, ever. List your car, sell it, and keep every dollar.
What can I list?
Cars, vans, utes, 4WDs and SUVs in any condition — running or not. You can sell the whole car, give it away for scrap, or part it out piece by piece.
How do buyers contact me?
Buyers enquire through the form on your listing, and their name, contact details and message come straight to you. You deal with them directly — no middleman.
Do I need a WOF or rego to list?
No. Cars without a current WOF or rego are welcome — just be upfront about it in your description so buyers know what they're getting.
What happens when my car sells?
Your listing gets a SOLD banner so buyers can see it's gone, and the enquiry form is switched off. Don't forget to sort change of ownership with NZTA at handover.
How does change of ownership work?
Both the buyer and the seller notify Waka Kotahi NZTA of the sale — it's a quick online form (or at an agent like NZ Post), free for the seller, and protects you both from future fines or fees on the vehicle.
